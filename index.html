About the Gathering

A RITUAL.
NOT JUST AN EVENT.

Fuego Creativo is a monthly creative fire circle for anyone who makes things — or wants to. Each session combines specialty coffee culture, personal creative development (inspired by Steal Like an Artist and The Artist's Way), and real human connection. No pitch decks. No networking bingo. Just your creative fire, a great cup, and a room full of people who get it.

Fuego Creativo Coffee is a specialty coffee movement built at the intersection of mentorship, origin knowledge, design culture, and creative storytelling — creating community around a creative fire.

Led by specialty coffee mentor Jimmy Avendano and Emmy Award-winning storyteller Lilian Cordova, the project connects origin coffee culture with the creator journey.

We believe coffee is more than a beverage. It's the pause before creating. The sip before starting. A quiet ignition of the artist inside you.
